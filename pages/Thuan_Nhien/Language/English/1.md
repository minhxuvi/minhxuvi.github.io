Mother Earth
</br>Verse by: Eileen Hutchins - Music by: Peter Patterson
</br>Mother earth, mother earth
</br>Take our seed and give it birth
</br>Father sun, gleam & glow
</br>Till the roots begin to grow
</br>Sister rain, sister rain
</br>Shed thy tear to swell the grain
</br>Brother wind, breath and blow
</br>Then the blade all green will grow
</br>Earth and sun, wind and rain
</br>Turn to gold the living grain
