# **1-Month "Teacher Training" Intensive**.

This schedule is designed to take you from "Blank Page" to "First Day of School," fully equipped with a localized Vietnamese curriculum and the artistic skills to teach it.

**Context:**

- **Goal:** Prepare for Grade 3 (The Rubicon Year).
- **Strategy:** Replace Hebrew/Chinese archetypes with Vietnamese equivalents (Thần Trụ Trời, Lạc Long Quân, Stilt Houses).
- **Daily Rhythm:** Morning for **Head** (Planning/Reading), Afternoon for **Hands** (Art/Skill).

---

### **The Master Curriculum Map (Your "North Star")**

| Block           | Traditional Waldorf    | **Your New Vietnamese Plan**                                                 |
| --------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Creation**    | Genesis / Creation     | **Thần Trụ Trời & The Cosmic Egg:** Separation of Heaven/Earth.              |

| **Grammar**     | Tower of Babel         | **The Singing Birds / The Scribe:** Naming the world; "Music" of the Tones.  |

### **Week 1: The Cosmic Beginning (Creation & Painting)**

**Goal:** Internalize the "Big Bang/Thần Trụ Trời" story and master **Wet-on-Wet Painting**.

- **Monday (Feb 9):**
- _Morning:_ Read **GA 311 (Kingdom of Childhood)** - Lecture 1. Write the outline for the story of "The Cosmic Egg."
- _Afternoon:_ **Painting:** Practice "Yellow Light surrounded by Blue Darkness." (Do this 3 times).

- **Tuesday (Feb 10):**
- _Morning:_ Read **GA 311** - Lecture 2. Compare Thần Trụ Trời’s labor to the 9-year-old’s need for work.
- _Afternoon:_ **Painting:** "Separation of Elements" (Red Fire, Blue Water, Green Earth).

- **Wednesday (Feb 11):**
- _Morning:_ Draft your **"Defense" Document - Part 1**. (Why Asian myths meet the developmental need for "Origin Stories").
- _Afternoon:_ **Chalkboard Drawing:** Practice drawing a "Pillar of Light" or a Mountain rising from the Sea. Use the _side_ of the chalk.

- **Thursday (Feb 12):**
- _Morning:_ Read **Rawson & Richter** (Grade 3 Section - Creation). Note the learning goals.
- _Afternoon:_ **Storytelling:** Practice telling the Thần Trụ Trời story aloud to an empty room. Focus on your voice dropping to a low, serious tone.

- **Friday (Feb 13):**
- _Morning:_ **Review:** Look at your paintings. Are the colors muddy? (If yes, use less water next time).
- _Afternoon:_ **Form Drawing:** Practice simple running forms (waves, mountains).

- **Weekend:** Rest & Nature Observation. Go to a rice field or river in Hội An. Watch how the water moves.

---

### **Week 2: The Earth & The Measure (Farming & Drawing)**

**Goal:** Prepare the Farming/Building blocks and master **Geometric/Technical Drawing**.

- **Monday (Feb 16):**
- _Morning:_ Read **Practical Advice (GA 294)** - Lecture 1-4 (Focus on writing/reading).
- _Afternoon:_ **Table Drawing (Crayon):** Draw a "Rice Plant" showing roots, stem, and grain. Focus on vertical energy.

- **Tuesday (Feb 17):**
- _Morning:_ Research **Shennong (Thần Nông)**. Write the story of him tasting the herbs (The hero who risks himself for knowledge).
- _Afternoon:_ **Measurement Practice:** Measure your own room using "Gang" (hand span) and "Sải" (arm span). Write down the numbers.

- **Wednesday (Feb 18):**
- _Morning:_ Draft **"Defense" Document - Part 2**. (Why "Stilt House" is better than "Noah's Ark" for Vietnamese spatial awareness).
- _Afternoon:_ **Chalkboard Drawing:** Draw a **Stilt House**. Focus on the structure: Pillars (Earth), Floor (Human), Roof (Sky).

- **Thursday (Feb 19):**
- _Morning:_ Plan the **Farming Field Trip**. Where in Hội An can you take them? (Vegetable village? Rice paddy?).
- _Afternoon:_ **Modeling (Clay/Beeswax):** Model a simple brick or a plow. Feel the weight of the earth.

- **Friday (Feb 20):**
- _Morning:_ **Review:** Does your Stilt House drawing look stable?
- _Afternoon:_ **Form Drawing:** Practice "Symmetry." Draw the left side of a leaf/vase, then the right side.

- **Weekend:** Visit a traditional house or carpenter if possible. Look at the joints.

---

### **Week 3: The Word & The Law (Grammar & Writing)**

**Goal:** Prepare the Grammar block and finalize your **School Presentation**.

- **Monday (Feb 23):**
- _Morning:_ Read **Rawson & Richter** (Grammar). Understand "Noun, Verb, Adjective."
- _Afternoon:_ **Writing Practice:** Practice your best "Chalkboard Handwriting." Write a short poem about the Wind (Verbs) and the Stone (Nouns).

- **Tuesday (Feb 24):**
- _Morning:_ Write the story of **The Scribe/The Bird Songs**. How did the Vietnamese people "catch" the wind (words) into a cage (writing)?
- _Afternoon:_ **Painting:** "The Mood of Grammar." Paint a solid Red block (Noun) and a swirling Yellow wave (Verb).

- **Wednesday (Feb 25):**
- _Morning:_ **Finalize "Defense" Document.** Print out your Comparative Table and Block Map.
- _Afternoon:_ **Rehearsal:** Present your curriculum change to a friend/partner. "I am using Thần Nông because..."

- **Thursday (Feb 26):**
- _Morning:_ Select your **Morning Circle Songs**. Find folk songs about farming (e.g., "Hạt Gạo Làng Ta" or similar).
- _Afternoon:_ **Music:** Practice singing the song while clapping the rhythm.

- **Friday (Feb 27):**
- _Morning:_ **Review:** Check your Block Plan. Do you have a story for every week?
- _Afternoon:_ **Free Art:** Paint or draw whatever you feel you are weakest at.

- **Weekend:** Rest deeply. You have finished the "Thinking" phase.

---

### **Week 4: The Rhythm & The Room (Practical Setup)**

**Goal:** Set up the classroom and get your body ready for the children.

- **Monday (March 2):**
- _Morning:_ **Classroom Setup.** Arrange the desks. Where does the light fall? Where do you stand?
- _Afternoon:_ **Supplies Check:** Do you have enough crayons, paper, chalk? Sharpen every crayon.

- **Tuesday (March 3):**
- _Morning:_ Write the **First Week's Schedule** (Day 1 to Day 5). Hour by hour.
- _Afternoon:_ **Chalkboard:** Draw the **First Day Drawing** (The Cosmic Egg/Sunrise) on the actual board in the classroom.

- **Wednesday (March 4):**
- _Morning:_ **Parent Meeting Prep.** What will you say to the parents on Day 1?
- _Afternoon:_ Walk through the "Morning Circle" in the empty classroom.

- **Thursday (March 5):**
- _Morning:_ Read **GA 311** - Last Lecture. Focus on the teacher's inner mood.
- _Afternoon:_ Meditate on the children. Visualize them entering the room.

- **Friday (March 6):**
- _Morning:_ **Rest.** Do nothing related to school.
- _Afternoon:_ **Rest.** Cook a good meal. Sleep early.
